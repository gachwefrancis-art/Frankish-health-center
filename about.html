FRANKISH HEALTH CENTER


About


Frankish Health centre is a brainchild from Francis Gachwe as the founder of the health centre.

It began as an outpatient level II facilty: providing basic health care services such as outpatient services, laboratory diagnosis, community health promotion strategies and education of the community,on how to prevent diseases to both the young and adults.

This built the foundation of the facility as a result of the commitment of the two nurses, in delivery health services to the community for their wellbeing.

The facilty in the community served as a vital organ on the community system and functionality providing quality and basic care to the children, aldults, both male and female regardless of their social, religious or economic status: following that God had granted them a gift of life we aimed to provide Quality care.

Mission: Provide quality health to all

vision:Promotion of health, Prevention of diseases and improving health status by providing patient centered care

Values:Provision of patient centered care.
